Nikola Tesla

The man who accomplished more in his lifetime than many of us could ever hope to achieve in a 100 lifetimes

Here's a time line of Nikola Tesla:

  • 1859 - Nikola Tesla is born
  • 1870 - Tesla's Brush With Death"
  • 1875 - Tesla Enrolls In College"
  • 1880 - Tesla Moves To Prague To Study Philosophy
  • 1881 - Tesla Begins His Career As Chief Electrical Engineer.
  • 1882 - Tesla Goes To Work For Edison.
  • 1885 - Receives a Ph.D. in Genetics and Plant Pathology
  • 1887 - Tesla Submits Some Of His First Patents.
  • 1890 - Tesla Re-imagines The Electric Motor
  • 1891 - Tesla Patents The Tesla Coil.
  • 1943 - Tesla's Death
